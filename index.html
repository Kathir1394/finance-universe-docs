Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

Thank you for using Finance Universe ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal financial data on our servers.

Finance Universe is designed as a local-first application. All data you enter into the app (including transaction details, budget information, and account balances) is stored locally on your device using an internal database. We do not have access to this data.

2. Data Export and Import

Finance Universe provides functionality to export your financial data to Excel files and import data from them. This feature is designed to give you full control over your data. The exported files are generated locally on your device. When you choose to share or save these files, they are handled by your device's standard sharing mechanisms. We do not intercept or upload these files to any external servers.

3. Receipt Scanning

The application includes a receipt scanning feature that uses on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit) to extract information from images you select. This processing happens entirely on your device. The images you scan are not uploaded to the cloud or shared with third parties.

4. Permissions

The application requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Storage/Photos: Required only if you choose to save exported files to your device or select receipt images for scanning.
  • Camera: Required only if you choose to take a photo of a receipt for scanning.

We do not request access to sensitive data such as your contacts, SMS messages, call logs, or precise location.

5. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party libraries for standard functionality (e.g., UI components, local notifications). These services are used strictly for technical purposes and do not collect personal information for tracking or advertising.

6. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security relies on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric lock.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
